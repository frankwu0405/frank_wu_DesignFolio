E-commerce Mobile App

AI-Driven Fashion Shopping App

A mobile shopping app with AI visualization to help users make confident purchasing decisions.

The Problem

Online fashion shoppers often struggle with decision-making due to the inability to visualize how clothes will fit and look on them. This uncertainty leads to high return rates and a frustrating user experience.

The Solution

We developed a user-centered mobile app that uses AI to allow shoppers to visualize clothing on a personalized avatar. By providing a more realistic and interactive shopping experience, we aimed to increase user confidence and reduce returns.

My Role & Process

  1. Conducted user interviews and market analysis to uncover user needs and frustrations in the current market.
  2. Created personas, journey maps, and usability scenarios based on research findings.
  3. Prototyped a digital shopping platform with AI visualization features to enhance decision-making.
  4. Presented findings and prototypes in the final showcase with positive feedback.
