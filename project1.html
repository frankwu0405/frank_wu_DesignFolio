EdTech Mobile App

Interactive Learning Tools for Remote Students

A digital STEM learning tool designed to engage students in rural and remote areas.

The Problem

Students in remote and rural areas often lack access to the same quality of STEM educational resources as their urban counterparts. This educational gap is compounded by a lack of engaging, interactive digital tools that can function in low-connectivity environments.

The Solution

We developed a mobile-first, gamified learning application that provides interactive STEM modules. The app is designed to work offline, ensuring accessibility for all students. By making learning feel like a game, we significantly increased student engagement and knowledge retention on complex STEM topics.

My Role & Process

  1. Led user research and interviews with students and teachers in rural areas.
  2. Synthesized insights into design requirements for a digital STEM learning tool.
  3. Prototyped and tested interactive, gamified learning modules with a focus on usability.
  4. Worked cross-functionally to iterate designs based on user testing.
